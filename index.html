About

I am a first semester student at the Texas A&M Univeristy pursuing Master of Science in Visualization.


My interests are Machine Learning, Virtual Reality and Computer Graphics. I enjoy using my skills to develop applications that aid people from various fields to streamline their work. I also love working with fundamental computer graphics libraries, blending my imagination and mathematical knowledge to re-create basic games and visuals.


My other interests include making Terracotta sculptures, playing soccer, travelling, trekking and photography.
